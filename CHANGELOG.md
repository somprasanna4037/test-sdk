## 1.0.2

* BoldDesk Mobile Support SDK, which allows seamless integration of Knowledge Base (KB) and Ticketing modules into your mobile applications.
You can now embed BoldDesk support features directly into your Android app with customizable themes, JWT-based authentication, and push notifications.
 
##### Key Features:
 
**Knowledge Base (KB) Module** – Browse articles directly in the app.
**Ticket Module** – Create and track support tickets.
**Authentication** – Secure login using backend-generated tokens.
**Custom Theming** – Light, Dark, and System theme support with custom branding.
**Font Customization** – Add custom fonts from your main app.
**Push Notifications** – Real-time ticket updates.
